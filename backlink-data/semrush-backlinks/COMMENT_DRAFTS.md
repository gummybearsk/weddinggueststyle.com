## Posting Guidelines

1. **Read the actual article** before posting. Adjust the draft to match the real content.
2. **Use your real name** or a natural-sounding name. Never use brand names as commenter names.
3. **Space out posts** - do not post more than 3-5 comments per day across all sites.
4. **Use the website field** in the comment form for your URL when available, rather than forcing it into the comment text. Only include the plain URL in the comment body when there is no website/URL field.
5. **Never post the same comment twice.** Pick one draft per domain, or customize further.
6. **Wait 24-48 hours** between commenting on sites within the same tier.
7. **Rotate commenter personas** - vary your first name, email, and tone.
